Introduction

Downloading Swedish elections data for years 2010, 2014 and 2018, and preview with shiny dashboard using RC-class by Marketos Damigos (marda352) and Christoforos Spyretos (chrsp415).

Usage Methods

el_2010 = elections("2010")
#> New names:
#> * LÄN -> LÄN...1

#> New names:

#> New names:
